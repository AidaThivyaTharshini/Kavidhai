Uyirae,

Thoorathil kaadhal, en idhayaththai nanaikiradhu,
Un ninaivugal en ullathil mithakindrana,
Un kannil naan kaanum kanavugal,
Un arugil illadha podhu, en uyirin sogangal.


Uravin unarvu, nammai inaikiradhu,
Un manadhil naan kaanum sindhanaigal,
En idhayathil neeye oru kathai,
Un kangalal kayalvizhi pole imaikiradhu.


Un nimidangalil naan kaanum amaidhi,
Un vaarththaigal en idhayathil oru kathai,
Thoorathil irundhaalum, en kaadhal neeye,
En idhayaththin kural, neeye en vaazhvu.


Un arugil naan oru kuzhandhai pol,
Un anbil naan vaazhkkiren,
Un vaarththaigal en manadhil,
Un thozh pol ver orr idam paadhukaapai illai endru naan unarthen.
